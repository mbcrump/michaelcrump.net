About

Hi, I’m Michael.

I’m a software developer and lifelong tinkerer. I use this site to document what I learn while working with code, developer tools, AI, cloud platforms, and whatever else has my attention.

I work in Developer Relations, where I focus on developer outreach through content, newsletters, events, YouTube, and other ways of helping technical communities learn, connect, and build.

I’ve written on the web for years. This version of the site is a reset: lighter, easier to maintain, and designed around publishing directly from the work itself.

The goal is useful specificity—short notes when a short note is enough, deeper walkthroughs when the details matter, and honest write-ups about the parts that did not go according to plan.
